How it works

Photo to portrait in four steps

Every order in Hamilton follows the same path, whether it's picked up or dropped off.

01

Send a photo

Share the picture you want engraved and pick a wood tone and size.

02

Approve the proof

You get a line-art preview before anything touches the wood.

03

It gets engraved

The laser cuts the portrait pass by pass, then it's sanded and sealed by hand.

04

Pickup or delivery

Ready in a few days — pick up locally in Hamilton or arrange delivery.
